About

Swarmnero is a true peer-to-peer social network with Monero payments built in. No servers, no relays, no middlemen. You own your data.

Key Concepts

Swarm ID
Your unique identifier on the network (64-character hex). Share this so others can follow you and see your posts. It's safe to share publicly.
Secret Key
Your private cryptographic key that controls your identity. NEVER share this - anyone with your Secret Key can post as you.
Mutual Follow
Direct messages require both users to follow each other. This prevents spam and ensures both parties want to communicate.

How P2P Works

There are no central servers. When you post, your content is stored in your local feed (a Hypercore append-only log). When someone follows your Swarm ID, they connect directly to you via the Hyperswarm DHT - a distributed hash table that helps peers find each other without any central coordination.

Tech Stack

  • Pear Runtime - Native P2P app
  • Hypercore - Signed append-only data logs
  • Hyperswarm DHT - Decentralized peer discovery
  • Monero - Private payments

Important Note

This app is designed for single-user use with multiple identities. All accounts on this device can manage (including delete) other accounts. Do not share this app installation with others. Use password protection on accounts you want to secure.
